Artists Reaching Out

Hello! We are a group of students at Columbia University working to bring art history and art lessons to local schools and shelters to provide an arts education for kids in our community. We are a Community Impact organization.

We are currently working in the Windham Center at 125th Street and the Regents Shelter at 110th. If you are a local school or shelter interested in partnering with us, please contact us at artistsreachingout@gmail.com !
